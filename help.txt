Controls for viewer:
    j to jump to a new page
    Escape to exit
    lefty:
        Move forward a page
            a or s
        Move back a page
            w or d
        Adjust forward
            q
        Adjust back
            e
    righty:
        Move forward a page
            Left or Down
        Move back a page
            Right or Up
        Adjust forward
            Comma
        Adjust back
            Period
